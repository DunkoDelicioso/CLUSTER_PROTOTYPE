What is Cluster Corp?

Cognitive Dissonance is one of the fundamental psychological drivers of our society today. It is essentially a discomfort experienced by holding two conflicting beliefs at the same time. This dissonance can be used as a powerful motivator to change ones life, or to rationalize past decisions. At Cluster Corp we use this concept to help you:

ASSIGN A VALUE TO YOUR LIFE

&

THEN LEVERAGE IT

By investing capital in intangible, hypothetical “molecules” with the pretense of self improvment you set the stakes for your own growth - one dollar at a time.
Molecules are ficticious particles you assign a psychic value to. These molecules are then sold as NFTs. By spending money on these assets you reinforce your goals by psychologically asserting that the money was not spent wastefully. The desire to rationalize this purchase drives the customer to “leverage their life” in the fullest sense possible. In the end the customer either wastes their money, or grows as a means to rationalize the purchase.

It’s simple & effective.
